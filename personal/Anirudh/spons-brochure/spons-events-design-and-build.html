Contraptions

Unlike other things at Shaastra, Contraptions turns things on its head when it asks you to complicate, convolute, and twist the simplest of actions with an infinite array of steps. This is a challenge to all those who think that simplest solutions are the way to go. The event forces you to rethink your beliefs, apply your engineering skills, and come up with the most complicated means to achieve your ends.


Fire N Ice

Engines purring to life, the hum of raw power in your hands, the powerful brakes screeching as you maneuver, the blue smoke from the exhaust. Sound familiar? Feel your heart beating faster? Come, unleash the RC beast within you as you steer through the toughest terrain conditions, make sharp turns and cross an array of obstacles. Be the king of the track with the nitro fuel propelled car engine.


Junkyard Wars

Want to make your own Atom, as Hugh Jackman did in Real Steel? Want to make the ultimate fighting machine out of seemingly useless junk? Or want to try your hand at what you could possibly make out of scrap metal junk? The ultimate hands-on experience at Shaastra attracting the largest crowds to watch teams battle it out in a neck to neck, adrenaline rushed, race against time competition.


Manual Robotics

Being one of the biggest of its kind in the entire country, this event tests the participant's ability to innovate, design, and improvise in order to emerge victorious. Show us your skill and expertise in designing a robot to do your bidding, or shall we say ours?

Master Builder

Curious about how you would do as a Structural Engineer? Want to learn more about taking your structural design idea from blueprints to a model? Unleash the civil engineer in you as we ask you to design anything from a bridge to a tower to an unbreakable wall. Think you can make gems out of stone? Showcase to us the Master Builder in you.

Robo Oceana

The perilous waves, the water obstacles, floating metal warriors, race against time... The water wars are here to enthral one and all present. Let your imagination loose and attain great creative heights as the next generation robot designers while you prove your mettle with your steel and plastic creations.

Robowars

Create your own ultimate fighting machine to beat, kick, punch and pummel the opponent to his demise. Yes, the brutish robot on robot fighting is on. Survival of the fittest? Or is it survival of the smartest? Come, seek and destroy.

Semi-Autonomous Robotics

A part of the mother of all robotics competitions in the country, semi autonomous robotics provides a platform for participants to unite and test their powers of ingenuity. Give brains to your bots. Let them be you.

Ultimate Engineer

Jack of all trades, master of none? We think not. Break the boundaries of engineering, prove your mettle as an engineer, show us your ability to think well outside “your” box as we throw both theoretical and practical problems at you. An inter-disciplinary delight. Come, be the Ultimate Engineer.
