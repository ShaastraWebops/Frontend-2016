Project X

The colossal event involves solving intriguing problems using ordinary materials. Exhibiting powers of creativity, enthusiasm and craftsmanship that everyone bears, the participation exceeds the 1000 mark. With such a huge number of people from diverse age groups, the Student Activity Center (SAC), where the event is held is one of the busiest hub at Shaastra.


Shaastra Cube Open

Affiliated to the World Cubic Association, it is one of the largest international speedcubing competitions in India, with global participation that smashes national records each year. A highlight event of Shaastra where world class cuber’s interact with young cubing enthusiasts.


Puzzle Champ

Testing analytical, tactical and logical abilities with set of thought provoking and mind boggling puzzles, the event promises an overwhelming participation on all three days, participants and audience alike.


How Things Work

Progress without understanding the fundamental concepts of a field is a myth. It is this understanding of the basic concepts that is tested in HTW. Garnering interest from avid quizzers to tech enthusiasts alike, this has been one of Shaastra’s mainstay events since the past decade. The prerequisites are only high school science, common sense and trust in one’s gut feeling. Promoting the spirit of scientific enquiry minus the boring equations or bland academic rigour, HTW is arguably the most unique quiz in the country.
