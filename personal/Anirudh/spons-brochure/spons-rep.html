Since it's inception, Shaastra's aim has been to explore activity outside the classroom and to spark creative, innovative and hand's-on approaches to a variety of problems from varying fields relating to technology. Over the years, the festival has evolved and now the grandeur and scale of events, is for all to behold. With thousands of people coming down to Chennai every year in January to witness this spectacle, Shaastra's reach is now beyond city, state and country and has a stake on international stage. Marquee events like IITM Research Expo and Industrial Open House remain the pillars of interaction between the student fraternity, industry and academia.
