Desmod

Design is the first step in any process which bridges the gap between new technology and human purposes. Desmod, the flagship event of the Department of Mechanical Engineering, challenges you to design CAD models to solve the issues faced in day to day affairs, to the extent of problems in complex industrial applications. Can you design a model which has the best aesthetics, ultimate durability, is cost effective and safe at the same being ergonomically favourable and user friendly? Desmod challenges that creative spontaneity within you. Let you imagination run riot and design a well-balanced novel solution, all this within a limited time!


Forensics

Are you a person who likes mysteries? Are you someone who is constantly on the lookout for clues and have an eye for detail? Welcome to the Department of Biotechnology’s Flagship event: Forensics, a perfect blend of puzzles, reasoning and science where you get a unique opportunity to experience the life of a detective. Don’t worry if you don’t know how to check for fingerprints because we will train you! Keep a good eye for detail, find clues, solve mysteries and nab the criminal!
