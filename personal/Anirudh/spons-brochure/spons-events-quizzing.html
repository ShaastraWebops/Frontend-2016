Shaastra Junior Quiz

An event truly meant for anyone - right from a layman to the geekiest individual out there. A basic knowledge of science along with a dash of common sense is all it takes! Conceived with the intention of developing a quizzing culture at the school level, the Shaastra Junior Quiz brings together the best quizzing minds from a plethora of cities all over the country for the grand finale in 2016. This event is guaranteed to be a crowd puller and has witnessed some extraordinary growth over the years.


Auto Quiz

For all the automobile freaks out there! The Auto quiz is one of the most innovative challenges at Shaastra, with questions ranging from the history of automobiles right to the future of it. A quizzing concoction of audio-visuals, auto trivia and engineering fundamentals, the auto quiz witnesses the most active audience participation and is bound to keep them at the edge of their seats. So, fasten your seat belts, rev up your engines and get ready to feel the rush!
