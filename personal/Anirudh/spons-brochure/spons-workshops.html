Chuck Glider Workshop

The two day Chuck glider workshop in association with ____ and Shaastra unfolds the intricacies of an integral part of aero modeling. The rudiments of chuck gliding in terms of functions of various parts of the glider, their design, assembling the parts, the science hidden in their movement, defining their trajectory etc. will be elucidated in the lucid manner. It captivates not only masses but also ignites the intellect of budding talent across the globe.


IoT Workshop

Line Following Hovercraft Workshop

Hovercraft , the most popular class of ACVs (Air Cushion Vehicles) , is one of the few ACVs that generate lift independent of thrust, as the name suggests, it hovers.Once challenged widely and noted with skepticism for its bold design, it was claimed to be just another outlandish model on paper, yet another flying car, with no practical application. Today , beating all odds, hovercraft is being used in a lot of rescue military operations.


Robo Oceana Workshop

Shaastra 2016 brings you a workshop on Marine Robotics in collaboration with ___, Shaastra Robo-oceana Workshop.This workshop gives you a basic understanding of Marine robotics - its components, mechanisms and principles. Build your first remote-controlled marine robot.
