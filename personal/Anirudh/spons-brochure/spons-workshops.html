Texas Instruments Workshop

C2000 Real Time Controller Workshop

The one day hands-on workshop covers the architecture of C2000 Real Time controller from Texas Instruments, data, program memory, peripheral organization and its special features. The peripherals include I/O Ports, Timers, PWM, A/D Converters, Universal Serial Interfaces and Comparators. The course is structured and tailored to meet the need of the engineering students to get a very good exposure of C2000 Real Time controllers which can be very well extrapolated to any other platform. An overview of e-PWM implementation on the motor control and LED lighting applications will be covered.
