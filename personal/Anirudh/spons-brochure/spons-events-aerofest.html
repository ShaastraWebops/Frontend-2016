Airshow

One of the best RC flying shows in the country, Airshow at Shaastra promises to be an eye candy for its audience who will be left spellbound by the night sky lit up by dazzling aeroplanes and helicopters twisting and swirling and soaring into breathtaking heights. The event will see some of the best aero-modelers from across the globe making the sky their canvas.


Aerobotics

Aerobotics is a fusion of the exciting disciplines of aero-modelling and robotics, challenging amateur as well as professional aero-modelers to develop intelligent aerial vehicles that respond to different stimuli during flight.


Wright Design

True to its name, this is a design intensive competition that challenges participants to design unpowered gliders without control systems. A thorough test of mathematics, flight dynamics and designing, the competition never ceases to amaze in terms of variety and innovation on show.
