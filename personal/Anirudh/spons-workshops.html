Line Following Hovercraft Workshop

Hovercraft, the most popular class of ACV's(Air Cushion Vehicles) that generate lift independent of thrust and as the name suggests, it hovers. Once challenged widely and noted with skepticism for its bold design, it was claimed to be another outlandish model on paper, yet another flying car with no practical application. Today, beating all odds, hovercraft is being used in all military rescue operations. This workshop is brought by Shaastra in association with ___________. It provides an opportunity for explorers to devise their own line follower hovercraft hands-on and to know all principles of aircraft governing the machine.
