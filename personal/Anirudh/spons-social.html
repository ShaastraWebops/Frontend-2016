Social Cause

The Pledge-A-Book Campaign is Shaastra's attempt to give back to society and create impacts in fields of education and literacy.This campaign aims to collect books which are donated by patrons of shaastra, and use these books to set up libraries for underpriveleged students.This campaign was flagged of by reknown science writer, Mr. Anil Ananthaswamy.
