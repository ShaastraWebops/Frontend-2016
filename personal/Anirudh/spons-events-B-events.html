Trader's Street

Trader's Street is a mock online trading competition where participants will showcase their trading skills using virtual money but real stock prices. Participants will trade on the virtual stock market platform during trading hours of BSE/NSE for a stipulated period of time And they will be judged based on their portfolio value(Cash + Stock Value) at the end of the trading period. Consigliere

Consigliere provides an unique opportunity for participants to diagnose a given live business world problem and interact with the people in the industry for collecting necessary information. It provides a platform for the participants to put themselves in the shoes of a consultant and suggest bold solutions for the existing problems faced by the companies operating in different sectors. initially the teams must compete in an online round to guarantee a spot for a chance to present their solution to high profile honchos at Shaastra.


Case Study

Case-study offers the perfect opportunity for students to apply themselves to a real world scenario. The event is designed to test both managerial skills as well as sound technical analysis to back it up. Analyze problems from different viewpoints and unleash your analytic skills. The selection procedure involves a written round and a group discussion among the competing teams. The selected teams are then expected to present a solution to a jury on a real world problem.


Big Data Challenge

The volume of data in our world has been ever expanding, and analyzing big data has become a key footing of competition, underpinning new waves of productivity growth, innovation, and consumer surplus. The Big Data Challenge tests you on how you go about using Big Data to address issues ranging from market investments to healthcare and come up with better and more beneficial decisions. The event will have a preliminary online round followed by a final round at Shaastra for the selected teams.
