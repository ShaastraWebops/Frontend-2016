Airshow

One of the best RC Flying Shows in the country, Airshow at Shaastra promises to be an eye-candy for its audience,who will be left spell-bound by the night sky lit up by dazzling aeroplanes and helicopters twisting and serving and soaring to breathtaking heights. The event will see some of the best aero-modelers from across the globe making the sky their canvas.
