Airshow:

One of the best RC Flying Shows in the country, Airshow at Shaastra promises to be an eye-candy for its audience,who will be left spell-bound by the night sky lit up by dazzling aeroplanes and helicopters twisting and serving and soaring to breathtaking heights. The event will see some of the best aero-modelers from across the globe making the sky their canvas.

Aerobotics:

Aerobotics is a fusion of the exciting disciplines of aero modelling and robotics,challenging amateur and professional aero-modellers to develop intelligent aero vehicles that respond to stimuli during flight.

Wright Design:

True to its name, this is a design intensive competition, that challenges participants to design unpowered gliders without control systems. A thorough test of Mathematics,flight dynamics and designing, the competition never ceases to amaze in terms of variety and innovation on show.
