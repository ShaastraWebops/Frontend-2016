Welcome to the IIT Madras Maker Summit 2016! The 4 day extravaganza set to change your perceptions and broaden your horizons from the 2nd to 5th of January 2016.

The theme for this year's symposium is "Makers". The beautiful experience of the Maker culture is set to blow your mind off. Our subtle message is, “Anyone can be maker”. Makers are techies, artists, creators, entrepreneurs. You.Are.A.Maker

The summit would include a panel discussion on the Maker culture in India, a keynote lecture, 2 unique workshops, a product design challenge including mentoring sessions for intense debate and discussion on the tracks – Transmedia storytelling, Life Hacks and Make a Difference.

In addition to the huge cultural exchange between the international and Indian students and the tremendous learning curve made possible due to a diverse set of participants, the IITM Summit promises to be a great experience for all.

Join us this January and be part of our Maker summit!

Panel Discussion

The growing Maker culture in India

The primary focus is on the developing Maker culture in India, where this movement is headed and what the opportunities available for young innovators and makers are. With a diverse panel of speakers from tech and non-tech backgrounds, the discussion will target the concept of interdisciplinary makers. The participants will have an exclusive opportunity to witness such a gathering of some of the most experienced makers.

Make-a-thon

The most interesting part of Maker Summit 2016. Get ready to join hands with other like-minded makers to observe, brainstorm and create a prototype from scratch. Participants have 4 days of time, basic workshop & makerspace facilities and a specific amount of resources to come up with something interesting, exciting and useful. Mentoring sessions will be arranged every day to help the participants and guide them through the exciting journey of surveying, ideating, designing and innovating. Exciting prizes and exclusive access to Makerspaces to be awarded to the winners. Stay tuned to our FB page to see the prizes in stock.

Workshops

Maker summit 2016 provides an opportunity to acquire a unique set of skills, through our workshop sessions.

1) 3D printing workshop: Ever seen a design on a software, come to life in just a couple of minutes. Ever tried printing a Bob, the minion figure? We at the Maker Summit provide the perfect chance to learn this simple yet necessary skill.

2) Laser cutting: Wouldn’t it be cool if one can cut a Batrang from a steel plate using a Laser? That is exactly what the participants will learn at our Laser cutting workshop.

Transmedia storytelling

The story experience is more than just reading a book, and can be told through various other media, such as games, events, cinema, television and nowadays, virtual reality. Define the story's purpose: ranging from educating the public about hygiene to creating a virtual pet and take the project forward through the storyboarding, film editing till prototyping of a product that may one day become the most exciting thing in the world.


Life Hacks

The potential for coming up with simple yet effective solutions for day-to-day issues, is immense. The development of a sensor based tap to save water, portable mobile phone charger and intelligent personal assistant in cell phones to prevent accidents are a few revolutionizing hacks. Explore various avenues after undertaking a complete market survey as part of this track.


Make a difference

The world as we know is plagued with thousands of problems. Extending an arm to help those who need it, is a huge responsibility. As students with problem solving skills and logic, we can make a difference and touch as many lives as possible. The primary focus will be on assistive technology.
