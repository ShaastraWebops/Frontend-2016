Tech and Innovation Fair

Tech and Innovation Fair at Shaastra 2016, IIT Madras is one of the best opportunities for tech enthusiasts to witness and experience cutting-edge technology and innovations that have taken place around the world.

The platform hosts the projects and exhibitions with novel ideas developed by the students across the globe. The developers get to exhibit their distinctive products to the eminent scientists, brilliant academicians and interact with the best brains of India.

Shaastra, IIT Madras hosts this fair aimed at promoting innovation in the world. As a part of it you can participate and exhibit your project or product to 30,000+ audience.

Chance to win INR 5000/- as cash prize.


Policy for participation in the Tech and Innovation Fair'16 -

The exhibit must be original or developed by you. The top exhibits would be selected and given an opportunity to be a part of exhibitions at Shaastra 2016, IIT Madras. The best exhibits will be given incentives and prizes.

In case your exhibit has a patent, please attach patent details in the mail.

There is no fees for registration. Shaastra sponsors only two participants for each project. Any extra participants have to pay for their arrangements. Anyone can be a part of the project. The person need not be pursuing any studies to be a part of the Tech and Innovation Fair'16.

The final selections will be done by IIT Madras.

Registration process to participate in Tech and Innovation Fair'16, IIT Madras -

  • Click on this link to download the format of the file to be submitted
  • Write all the details in a google document and share it with exhibitions@shaastra.org with commenting rights
  • Name the document in the following format "TAIF'16_<Project Name>" and put the subject as "Tech and Innovation Fair 2016"
  • The final selection list will be intimated to you by email if you’re selected. In case of multiple submissions your project might not get selected
  • The right of selection solely rests with IIT Madras and in case of any discrepancies the organizer's decision is final
  • Deadline for submission is 10th January, 2016
