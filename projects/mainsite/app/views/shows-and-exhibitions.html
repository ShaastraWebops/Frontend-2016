Envisage

A novel techno cultural show specializing in designing your dreams in the field of science and technology. It is a marvel of awesomeness as the transcendental fusion of art and technology pleasures your visual senses and stupefies you with audio effects. Being the only student organized event of its kind in the whole country, it gives a new meaning to your idea of entertainment and turning projects into a memory you will long cherish!

The work of science can be enigmatic. Have your sight deceived by animations shown using just a strip of LEDs. It was a new groundbreaking performance.

This year it is going be even BIGGER & BETTER! Join us at Shaastra 2016 and get ready to share the awe!

Air show

One of the biggest RC flying shows in the country, with planes and helicopters all twisting and twirling and fliers using the sky as the canvas for their masterpieces. Previous editions of Shaastra have witnessed some of world's best aero modellers like Wolfgang Krahofer, Gernot Bruckmann from Austria and 14 years old Jase Dussia from USA. Shaastra Air Show never failed in leaving its spectators in wonder!
