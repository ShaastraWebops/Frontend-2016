Welcome to Shaastra-IITM Summer (SIS) fellowships


Want an Internship at IITM ? Here's a chance for you to get one by winning an event in Shaastra.

Shaastra 2016 is glad to launch Shaastra-IITM Summer Fellowships for the first time ever. Through this initiative, winners of some specific events are given fellowships and a chance to do internship* at IIT Madras during Summer 2016. These winners are also given a 1-day orientation before commencement of their internship.


The events that are currently a part of this program are:

· Shaastra Aerial Robotics Contest

· Start-Up Wars

· Mixels

· Machine learning event

· Vaayu Shakthi

· Shaastra Circuit Design Challenge

· Manual Robotics

· Big Data Challenge

· Code to optimise

· Humanoid

· Research Expo - Biotech

Check this page regularly for more events that would become a part of this program.

Contact Balasaravanan TK (bala@shaastra.org) for any queries related to this program

Terms and Conditions,

· Internships are given based on availability of intern positions in different labs at IIT Madras

· Shaastra only provides an interface for the winners to get an internship at IIT Madras

· Fellowships entitles a stipend amount of 8000 Rs. that the student would receive while doing the internship at IIT Madras

· Fellowship can only be deemed after successful completion of the internship at IIT Madras

· Internship duration is 6-8 weeks. However, it can vary based on respected lab's requirements

· This fellowship is different from the regular Institute fellowship

· Winners have a choice to decide the lab at IITM based on availability at that time

· Certificates will be provided at the completion of internship
