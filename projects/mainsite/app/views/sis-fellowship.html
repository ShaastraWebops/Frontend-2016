Welcome to Shaastra-IITM Summer (SIS) fellowships


Want an Internship at IITM ? Here's a chance for you to get one by winning an event in Shaastra.

Shaastra 2016 is glad to launch the Shaastra-IITM Summer Fellowships for the very first time. Through this initiative, winners of some specific events are given fellowships and a chance to do an internship* at IIT Madras during the Summer of 2016. These winners are also given a one day orientation before commencement of their internship.

Terms and Conditions,

· Internships are awarded based on availability of intern positions in different labs at IIT Madras

· Shaastra only provides an interface for the winners to get an internship at IIT Madras

· Fellowships entitles a stipend amount of Rs. 8000 that the student will receive after successfully completing the internship

· Internship duration is 6-8 weeks. However, it can vary depending on each lab's requirements

· This fellowship is different from the regular Institute fellowship

· Winners will have a choice of the lab they wish to intern at, subject to the availability of intern positions

· Certificates will be provided on completion of the internship
